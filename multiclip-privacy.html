1. Introduction

MultiClip ("we", "our", or "the extension") is a clipboard manager browser extension developed by SY5TEM N3XT. This privacy policy explains how MultiClip handles your data.

2. Data Collection

What We Collect:

NOTHING. MultiClip does not collect, transmit, or store any user data on external servers.

What MultiClip Stores Locally:

The following data is stored only on your device using your browser's local storage:

  • Text clips you copy
  • Images/screenshots you copy
  • Your profile names and organization preferences
  • Your starred/favorited clips
  • Your customization settings (colors, themes, preferences)

Important: All this data stays on YOUR device. It never leaves your computer. We cannot access it. No one else can access it.

3. Data Storage

All clipboard history and settings are stored using:

  • Chrome Local Storage API - Data stored in your browser's local storage
  • Location: On your device only
  • Persistence: Data remains until you clear it or uninstall the extension

4. Data Usage

Your clipboard data is used solely for:

  • Displaying your clipboard history in the extension popup
  • Allowing you to search and organize your clips
  • Enabling you to paste previously copied content
  • Organizing clips across your custom profiles

6. Permissions Explained

MultiClip requires the following browser permissions:

clipboardRead

Required to capture content when you copy (Ctrl+C) so the extension can save it to your clipboard history.

clipboardWrite

Required to paste saved clips back to web pages when you click on them.

storage

Required to save your clipboard history locally in browser storage so it persists between sessions.

contextMenus

Required to provide right-click menu options (Edit, Preview, Star, Delete) on your saved clips.

activeTab

Required to paste content into the active webpage when you click a saved clip.

None of these permissions are used to collect or transmit data.
